### Algorithme de Karatsuba ###

$$(a × 10^k + b)(c × 10^k + d) = ac × 10^{2k} + (ac + bd – (a – b)(c – d)) × 10^k + bd$$

Recursion:

$$T(n) = 3T(n/2) + cn + d$$

Complexité : $$n^(log_23)$$
